Face to Face Classes vs Online Classes

    Face to Face Classes

  • Pros: Direct interaction, structured environment, social engagement, hands-on learning.
  • Cons: Fixed schedule, commute required, limited accessibility for remote learners.

    Online Classes

  • Pros: Flexible schedule, accessible from anywhere, diverse resources, cost-effective.
  • Cons: Less interaction, potential technical issues, requires self-discipline, limited practical experience.
